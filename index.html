CPM.AnglicanBook
Resources for The Church of the Provence of Myanmar.
This CPM.AnglicanBook app includes:
  • lyrics of all hymns, currently sung in CPM, which also contain over 14 hour long audio tracks of all those songs in order that the congregation can sing along the hymns,
  • All the entire book of common prayer in Myanmar, Karen, Kachin, Khumi_Chin Languages which has been developed to be used at convenience,
  • The Holy Bible with the audio
  • Available in Myanmar, Sagaw Karen, Kachin and Khumi Chin
